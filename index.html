Hi, I’m Jenna Ryan and I am a creative designer from the East Coast now based in Southern California. I have been expanding my design skills which include fashion, graphic and textile design to include visual design and a knowledge of web development.

I am a physically active person which is why my love of fitness and my career in fashion design intersected perfectly and led me to design activewear. I enjoy researching, creating and testing functional clothing with problem solving solutions for real people.
